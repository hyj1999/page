简 介：Obadiah Stane 是托尼·斯塔克的商业伙伴，也是他父亲霍华德·斯塔克的好朋友。霍华德去世后，斯坦恩成为斯塔克工业公司的临时首席执行官，直到托尼不久后正式接任。史丹对年轻的史塔克的嫉妒导致他与十环联手组织了一次失败的暗杀企图，以便史丹可以再次成为首席执行官。他进一步用史塔克工业的武器武装十环，以促进他们在饱受战争蹂躏的古尔米拉的战役。当 Pepper Potts 揭露他与恐怖分子的关系时，Stane 创造了一件盔甲并取名为 Iron Monger 并试图杀死 Stark 本人，导致他自己死亡。
OVERVIEW：Obadiah Stane was the business partner of Tony Stark and a good friend of his father, Howard Stark. After Howard’s death, Stane became interim CEO of Stark Industries until Tony took over officially shortly after. Stane’s jealousy of the younger Stark led him to join forces with the Ten Rings to organize a failed attempt to assassinate him so Stane could become CEO once again. He further armed the Ten Rings with Stark Industries weapons, to facilitate their campaign in the war-torn Gulmira. When his involvement with terrorists was exposed by Pepper Potts, Stane created an armor and adopted the name Iron Monger and attempted to kill Stark himself, leading to his own death.

详细介绍

Obadiah Stane was the business partner of Tony Stark and a good friend of his father, Howard Stark. After Howard's death, Stane became interim CEO of Stark Industries until Tony took over officially shortly after. Stane's jealousy of the younger Stark led him to join forces with the Ten Rings to organize a failed attempt to assassinate him so Stane could become CEO once again. He further armed the Ten Rings with Stark Industries weapons, to facilitate their campaign in the war-torn Gulmira. When his involvement with terrorists was exposed by Pepper Potts, Stane created an armor and adopted the name Iron Monger and attempted to kill Stark himself, leading to his own death.
奥巴迪亚·斯坦是托尼·斯塔克的商业伙伴，也是他父亲霍华德·斯塔克的好朋友。霍华德去世后，斯坦成为斯塔克工业公司的临时首席执行官，直到托尼不久正式上任。斯坦对小斯塔克的妒忌使他和《十枚戒指》联手组织了一次失败的暗杀行动，以便斯坦再次成为首席执行官。他进一步用斯塔克工业公司的武器武装了十环，以促进他们在饱受战争蹂躏的古尔米拉的战役。当他与恐怖分子的牵连被佩珀·波茨揭露时，斯坦制造了一件盔甲，并改名为铁商，试图自杀，导致斯塔克死亡。
