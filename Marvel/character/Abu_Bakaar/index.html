详细介绍

Abu Bakaar was a member of the terrorist group called the Ten Rings who detained Tony Stark in an effort to make him build new weapons for them. Bakaar was the main contact for Stark while Ho Yinsen worked as his translator; Bakaar ordered Stark to build them a version of the Jericho missile. When Stark refused, Bakaar had him tortured until he agreed to help them. Eventually, Raza took over Bakaar's role as Stark's contact.
阿布·巴卡尔是恐怖组织“十环”的成员，该组织拘留托尼·斯塔克，试图让他为他们制造新武器。巴卡尔是斯塔克的主要联系人，何银森是他的翻译；巴卡尔命令斯塔克为他们制造一种杰里科导弹。当斯塔克拒绝时，巴卡尔折磨他，直到他同意帮助他们。最终，拉扎接管了巴卡尔作为斯塔克联系人的角色。

Bakaar was among the militants working for the Ten Rings in Gulmira, the small Afghan region that was home to Ho Yinsen. Bakaar led a group of the insurgents that were killing and trucking people out of the region when Iron Man arrived in his newly constructed Mark III Suit. Iron Man killed many of the Ten Ring members there, but left Bakaar alive, turning him over instead to the mercies of the residents of Gulmira.
巴卡尔是在古尔米拉（Gulmira）为“十环”组织工作的激进分子之一，古尔米拉是阿富汗的一个小地区，也是何银森的家乡。当铁人身穿新建造的马克三世（MarkIII）西装抵达时，巴卡尔（Bakaar）领导的一群叛乱分子正在杀害并用卡车将人们运出该地区。铁人杀死了那里的十名魔戒成员中的许多人，但却让巴卡尔活着，把他交给了古尔米拉居民的仁慈之手。

Abu Bakaar was brutal, sadistic, and apathetic in his goals, and lacked any empathy for innocent people, attempting to kill a father because he embraced his son in Gulmira. He was greatly loyal to Raza and followed any order given to him, going as far to have Tony Stark tortured. In addition, he was manipulative, trying to persuade Stark to make the missile, and guaranteeing him freedom once he was done. When Stark attacked the Ten Rings in Gulmira, he hid away and was calling in reinforcements, showing himself to be cowardly.
阿布·巴卡尔（AbuBakaar）残忍、虐待狂，对自己的目标漠不关心，对无辜的人没有任何同情心，他试图杀害一位父亲，因为他在古尔米拉拥抱了自己的儿子。他对拉扎非常忠诚，遵守任何命令，甚至对托尼·斯塔克施以酷刑。此外，他还善于操纵，试图说服斯塔克制造导弹，并保证他一旦制造完成就获得自由。当斯塔克袭击古尔米拉的十环时，他躲了起来，并要求增援，表现出自己的懦弱。

In a deleted scene from Iron Man, Bakaar was executed by Raza, after he joined Tony Stark and Ho Yinsen for a board game. This cut footage contradicts the events of the final film.
在《钢铁侠》删除的一个场景中，巴卡尔在加入托尼·斯塔克和何银森的棋盘游戏后被拉扎处决。这段剪辑片段与最后一部电影的情节相矛盾。

Archive footage of Abu Bakaar from Iron Man was used in the trailers of Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings.
《钢铁侠》中阿布·巴卡尔的档案录像被用于《尚志》和《十环传说》的预告片中。
